Frequently Asked Questions

Is the video compressor really free?
Yes! Our video compressor is completely free. No premium versions, no hidden costs, no credit card required. We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone.
Are my videos uploaded to a server?
No! All video compression happens directly in your browser using WebAssembly technology. Your videos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What video formats are supported?
We support MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM, FLV, and many other common video formats. You can also choose your preferred output format after compression.
Will compression reduce video quality?
You can control the balance between file size and quality using our compression slider. Higher compression levels significantly reduce file size while maintaining acceptable quality.
Is there a file size limit?
We recommend videos up to 500MB for optimal browser performance. For larger videos, consider using our server-side processing option available on DigitalOcean.
